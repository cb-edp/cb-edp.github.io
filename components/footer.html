Footer.

Closing an email is always done by the footer of that mail. The footer contains necessary information for the user, with links to unsubscribe, get in touch with the customer service or read up on legal documents like our privacy policy. We also often use the footer to get customer feedback via Close Alert.

Essential components

These components are always included in an email:

Content bar

A branded bar containing some general links to the customer service, stores and the app.
