Body.

The body contains the 'meat' of our email. Here we put blocks that contain the main content of the email, like information, promotions and branding items. All blocks in the body are optional and can be configured in many different ways.

Essential elements

The body of an email has no essential elements.

These elements can be used in emails, but are not necessary:
